The Higher Education Courses Recommendation is the result of a dissertation project. Its main goal was to assess how to use alumni and job offers information to recommend the most suitable courses to be closer to achieve a specific career. Here, we present a proof-of-concept with Computer Science as the target discipline.

You can navigate through different areas of expertise in Computer Science through the drop-down and see the recommended courses accordingly to the area chosen. Apart from higher education courses, in some expertise areas, we also recommend online courses that can be suitable for that particular area.
